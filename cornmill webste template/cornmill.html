Welcome to Cornmill Cottage & Apartments

The Cornmill Cottages are located in the Northern Plain of the Isle of Man, well known for its sunnier climes amid the quiet unspoilt beautiful Manx countryside at Lhen Bridge. The former 19th century water driven cornmill and Millers Cottage have been tastefully renovated and modernised to a very high standard, whilst still retaining many of the original features with old-style charm. They are at the periphery of a modern working arable f arm, but with their own separate entrance.
